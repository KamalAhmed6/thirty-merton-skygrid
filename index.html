04 · Crew Deployment Model

How We Staff. How We Scale.

SafeCan's deployment model is built for high-rise sequencing — crews are staged floor by floor, and scaled up or down based on construction turnover pace.

🎯
Dispatch Protocol
Every crew is dispatched through our automated system — 24 hours before mobilization. Crew leads receive a full brief with job number, floor assignment, phase, supervisor contact, and check-in link.
Auto-dispatch 24 hrs prior
Job # + floor assignment in brief
SMS + Email confirmation
Supervisor escalation path included
📍
GPS Check-In / Check-Out
Every crew member checks in on arrival via our live web app — capturing GPS location, timestamp, and photo documentation. Missed check-ins trigger an automated alert within 15 minutes.
GPS-stamped arrival + departure
Photo upload per area completed
15-min missed check-in alert
Real-time supervisor dashboard
📊
Live Operations Monitor
Skygrid gets access to our Field Operations Monitor — a live dashboard showing every active crew, floor status, phase progress, and area completion across the entire project in real time.
Live crew tracking per floor
Phase completion % by area
Downloadable daily logs
Client-facing progress view
📅
Rolling Floor Sequencing
Rather than waiting for full-building handover, SafeCan sequences crews floor by floor as construction turns over — maximizing productivity and compressing the overall timeline.
Starts at podium as floors clear
3 phase streams run concurrently
Crew count scales with turnover rate
Weekly schedule reviews with GC
⚡
Scalable Crew Model
Our crew model scales from a minimum of 6 workers to a peak of 20 depending on phase overlap and construction handover pace. Surge capacity can be activated within 48 hours.
Min 6 / standard 8 / peak 20 crew
48-hr surge capacity activation
Phase overlap maximizes speed
Dedicated site supervisor on-call
🛡️
Quality Assurance
Every floor is signed off before the next phase begins. Our QA checklist is completed digitally, with photographic evidence, and a floor-level sign-off submitted to Skygrid before PDI.
Digital floor sign-off per phase
Photo documentation per area
Deficiency flagging + resolution
GC/client walkthrough support
05 · Technology Stack

The Infrastructure Behind the Work

SafeCan runs on a purpose-built operations platform — not generic software. Every tool is designed for post-construction cleaning at scale.

Dispatch + Scheduling
Auto-Dispatch Engine
Reads our Google Calendar "Contractors on Site" feed and automatically generates crew briefs, dispatches them via email + SMS, and logs every deployment. Zero manual work per dispatch.
Auto-Trigger 24-hr Pre-Brief Calendar-Linked Job # Tracking
Check-In System
SafeCan Field Check-In App
A live web app deployed on Google Apps Script — accessible from any phone without installation. Captures GPS, photo, arrival time, and area. Every submission is logged to a live Google Sheet and triggers a supervisor notification.

Live Dashboard
Field Operations Monitor
A real-time operations dashboard showing every active job, crew check-in status, floor completion, area progress, and photo feed. Updated live from field submissions. Skygrid can be granted a read-only view at any time.
