Why This Matters

The cleaning contractor you choose affects more than cleanliness.

A missed PDI, a delayed floor sign-off, or a crew that doesn't show — these aren't just cleaning problems. They hold up trades, push back occupancy dates, and put your project timeline at risk. SafeCan is built specifically to prevent that.

We operate on the same floor-by-floor logic that your construction team uses. We sequence with your GC, we report in real time, and we don't need to be chased. When a floor is ready, we're already on it.

Assumptions & Exclusions

What's included. What isn't.

This proposal is scoped based on the assumptions below. Changes to site conditions, construction sequencing, or scope may result in a revised proposal.

Assumptions
Construction team provides floor-by-floor turnover schedule minimum 48 hours in advance.
SafeCan has access to freight elevators during scheduled working hours (7am–5pm Mon–Fri).
Temporary power and water available on each floor during cleaning phases.
All construction debris is removed by trades before rough clean begins.
Parking levels (P1–P4) are excluded from this scope and priced separately if required.
Skygrid designates one project contact for scheduling coordination and sign-offs.
Pricing is firm for 60 days from the proposal issue date (May 7, 2026).
Exclusions
Cleaning of parking levels P1–P4 (available as add-on scope).
Exterior window cleaning above ground level (requires specialized equipment — quoted separately).
Supply of cleaning materials and consumables unless explicitly stated.
Waste disposal beyond standard bagging and placement at designated site waste areas.
Restoration or repair of any surface damage caused by other trades.
Permits, engineering, or any work outside the scope defined in this proposal.
Work outside the Greater Toronto Area or outside normal business hours without prior written approval.
Change Management

When scope changes, here's how we handle it

Post-construction projects evolve. SafeCan has a clear, documented process for scope changes so there are never surprises on either side.

Written Change Orders Only. Any change to the approved scope of work — additional floors, added phases, expanded areas, or schedule changes — is documented in a written Change Order before work proceeds. No verbal authorizations.

48-Hour Turnaround. SafeCan will provide a written Change Order with updated pricing and timeline impact within 48 hours of any scope change request from Skygrid.

No Unauthorized Work. SafeCan will not perform work outside the approved scope without a signed Change Order. This protects both parties and ensures clean invoicing at every phase.

Schedule Impacts Flagged Immediately. If any change to scope will impact the 16-week handover timeline, Skygrid will be notified in writing within 24 hours with a revised schedule and options.
